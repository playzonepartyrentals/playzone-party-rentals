1. Reservations, Payment & Cancellations

  • A completed booking form confirms your requested date and package, subject to Company approval.
  • Payment is due at or before delivery / pickup, unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  • Cancellations less than 48 hours before the event may be subject to a fee at the Company’s discretion.

2. Weather Policy

  • The Company may cancel or reschedule in cases of unsafe weather (lightning, heavy storms, extreme winds, etc.).

  • Once equipment has been delivered or picked up, the rental is considered in use and no refunds are given for changes in weather.

3. Delivery, Pickup & Access

  • Renter is responsible for providing clear access to the yard or setup area (no locked gates, vehicles blocking, excessive debris, or pet waste).

  • Renter agrees not to move equipment after the Company has dropped it off or set it up.

4. Supervision & Safe Use

  • No rough play, tackling, or unsafe behavior should be allowed, especially with throwing games and Nerf equipment.
  • Renter is responsible for ensuring that all participants are playing safely and respectfully.

  • Renter may be charged for lost or damaged items, including missing pieces, broken parts, or excessive damage.
  • Excessive mud, spills, or damage from misuse may result in additional cleaning or replacement fees.

6. Nerf & Higher Activity Games

  • For Nerf kits and similar active games, eye protection must be worn as directed.
  • Renter is responsible for making sure players understand safe distances, appropriate targets, and respectful play.
  • Do not aim at faces, heads, or non-participants.

7. Add-Ons & Setup Service for Games

  • Setup Service for Games — $20: For yard game rentals, Renter may choose an optional Setup Service during booking. This includes:
